Web3.0

Web3.0 uses blockchain technology to create a more decentralized internet, where data and applications are not controlled by a few large companies.

Web3.0 allows for more user-centric identity management, where users can control their own data and privacy.

AI

AI is being used in a wide range of industries and applications, from healthcare to finance to transportation, and is having a significant impact on society.

Breakthroughs: advances in deep learning and large language models have led to breakthroughs in natural language processing, computer vision, and other areas of AI.

AR & VR

AR & VR provide immersive and interactive experiences that can transport users to new worlds and environments.

AR & VR are used in gaming, entertainment, education, manufacturing, medicine, and other industries, and are becoming increasingly popular.
